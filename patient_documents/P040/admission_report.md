## Chief Complaint & Admission History
The patient is a 50-year-old male with an extensive baseline history of complex physical trauma and a severe intolerance to non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs who was admitted to the orthopedic service following an emergency room presentation for acute shoulder and knee injuries sustained during a reported accidental fall down a rocky incline.
